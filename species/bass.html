Black bass

Description

The black bass, or larguemouth bass(Micropterus salmoides),
is a freshwater fish native to North America, known for its
aggressive behavior and popularity in spor fishing.

This fish has a greenish body with a lighter belly and dark
spots along his sides. Typically found in lakes and rivers,
it preys on smaller fish, insects, and amphibians. His size
and strength make it a prized catch among anglers

Characteristics

  • Length: 35-75 cm
  • Weight: 0,45-7 kg
  • Diet: Carnivorous
